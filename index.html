Food & vermouth on Calle de la Palma

🍷 El Amor Hermoso · C. de la Palma 62 — frozen vermouth, tapas and crystal-bread sandwiches, fun corner bar.

🍷 Bravo Wine Bar · C. de la Palma 63 — natural & local wines by the glass, knowledgeable staff, small tasting room.

🍴 Casa Tabacos · C. de la Palma 69 — old tobacco shop turned gastro-taberna: ensaladilla, croquetas, tortilla, Spanish wines.

🍷 Casa Camacho · San Andrés 4 — open since the 1920s, barrels behind the bar, vermouth on tap poured straight from the source. A Malasaña classic.

Cold beer

🍺⭐ La Ardosa · Colón 13 — legendary Malasaña bar since 1892. Guinness straight from its own tap, cold Mahou on draught, free croquetas caseras with every drink. The real thing.

6:30 pmWalkLa Latina & Plaza Mayor stroll

L5 La Latina Walk north through Sol and Gran Vía before looping down to La Latina and Plaza Mayor.

⭐ Puerta del Sol — Madrid's km 0 and beating heart. The Bear & Madroño statue, the clock tower that rings in the New Year. Always buzzing; you pass right through from the hotels.

🏙️ Gran Vía — Madrid's grand boulevard: Beaux-Arts and Art Deco towers, neon signs, the Telefónica building. 5 min walk north from Sol — stroll west toward Plaza de España and double back.

🏺 Plaza Mayor — the great Habsburg square; colonnaded arcades, Felipe III on horseback, street performers. Head south from Sol 5 min to reach it.

🍺 Cervecería La Paloma 85 · Calle de Toledo 85 — gambas a la plancha + free boquerones with every drink. Tin counter, cash only, pure old Madrid.

↓  ~20 min taxi to Vallecas

11:00 amWalkSabatini Gardens + Campo del Moro

L2 ÓperaL5 Ópera Formal gardens behind Royal Palace — very photogenic, locals sometimes dancing. Walk around to Campo del Moro for the hidden royal gardens with swans. Very quiet, locals' secret. Allow ~1.5 hrs.

👑⭐ Palacio Real · Calle de Bailén s/n — western Europe's largest royal palace, right here. Opulent state rooms, Caravaggio and Velázquez originals inside. Book timed entry online (€14). Allow 1.5 hrs.

⛪ Catedral de la Almudena · Calle de Bailén 10 — right next to the Royal Palace; Madrid's cathedral, bright Neo-Gothic interior, free to enter. Easy add-on before or after the gardens.

🏛️ Templo de Debod · Parque del Oeste — a genuine 2,200-year-old Egyptian temple (gifted by Egypt), ~10 min north of the gardens. Free to walk the grounds; one of Madrid's best sunset spots.

🍢 De Princesa · Calle de la Princesa 51, Argüelles — tapas spot ~15 min north of the gardens, if anyone wants to keep grazing in this direction.

🍺 Casa Labra · Calle de Tetuán 12, Sol — Madrid institution since 1860. Cold draft beer with a free piece of fried bacalao. Standing room only, wood panelling, pure old city. 5 min walk from the palace toward Sol.

L4 Colón Recoletos 10, Salamanca. The tortilla de patatas — genuinely life-changing. Also: huevos rotos con trufa, foie pintxos. Booked 1:15pm for 4 · €80 deposit paid

🛍️ Barrio de Salamanca — you're already here. Madrid's most elegant neighbourhood: Calle Serrano and Calle Goya for designer boutiques and beautiful Belle Époque streets. Easy wander after lunch before heading to El Retiro (a 10 min walk east).

🍺 La Daniela · Jorge Juan 17 — classic Salamanca taberna, cold draft beer, great cocido madrileño and croquetas. The neighbourhood's traditional anchor bar.

🦐 Los Crustáceos · Calle Francisco Zea 11, Guindalera — Tiny seafood bar, fresh Galician shellfish on the plancha. Under new ownership since 2025 — still open.

🏡 Barrio de Ibiza · east of the park — slip out the east gate into a quiet, very local residential barrio: leafy streets, the Mercado de Ibiza, neighbourhood tabernas. ~5 min from the park.

🍷 Bodegas Casas · Av. Ciudad de Barcelona 23, Pacífico — 100-year-old bodega on the south edge of El Retiro, classic vermut on tap and no-frills tapas. Good stop on the way out of the park.
